Staying Safe During the Pandemic

1. Wash your hands. Make sure to thoroughly lather your hands with soap and scrub for 20 seconds.

2. Wear a face mask. Cover your mouth and nose when using a face mask.

3. Keep your distance. Try to keep a distance of 6 feet from others.
